Fudge Brownies

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter
  • chocolate
  • flour
  • pinch of salt
  • baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix the wet ingredients
  2. Mix the dry ingredients
  3. Cut the choclate into chunks
  4. mix all ingredients, but don't overmix!
  5. Form 3cm balls from the dough and put them on a baking sheet
  6. Bake the cookies for 10 minutes at 180C
