Sauerkraut

Ingredients

  • cabbage
  • salt
  • Spices (juniper berry, bay leave, etc.)

Steps

  1. cut the cabbage into stripes
  2. add 10g salt for 1kg of cabbage
  3. for 10 minutes, squeeze the cabbage with your hands. There should be a lot of juice coming out
  4. fill the cabbage into glasses so there is just little air
  5. All the hard work is done! Now, you just need to wait for a week.
  6. Try your yummy delicious cabbage
